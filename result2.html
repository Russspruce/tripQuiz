Vacation Quiz Results

Las Vegas

Since you don't mind traveling, travel big and go all the way out and party it up in Las Vegas! What better way to go wild and crazy than to party it up in Sin City! With casinos, bars, restaurants, and attractions galore. You have no restrictions when it comes to money and fun, so you can go ahead and try to win big, or go out and see some of the amazing performances throughout the city. There is no end in sight in this city where the neon lights are shining bright. Sure there is a bit more of a risk, but you will have fun from beginning to end.
